<p><p>The Semiconductor Ultra High Purity (UHP) valves market includes various types designed for critical applications in semiconductor manufacturing. Diaphragm valves offer excellent sealing and control for corrosive gases. Bellows valves provide leak-tight performance and resistance to contamination. Ball valves ensure quick operation and low pressure drop, while butterfly valves are space-efficient and suitable for large flows. Door valves are commonly used for system isolation, and angle valves assist in space-constrained environments. Teflon valves offer chemical resistance, ensuring purity and integrity. Other specialty valves cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
