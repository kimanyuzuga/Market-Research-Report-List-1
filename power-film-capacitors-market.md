<p><p>The Power Film Capacitors Market is categorized based on the type of dielectric films used. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) Film Capacitors offer good electrical characteristics at a lower cost, suitable for general applications. PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate) Film Capacitors provide enhanced thermal and electrical performance compared to PET. Other types may include films like PVC or polystyrene, catering to specific needs.</p></p>
