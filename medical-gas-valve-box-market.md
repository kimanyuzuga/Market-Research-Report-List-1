<p><p>The medical gas valve box market is categorized into two main types: single zone valve boxes and multiple zone valve boxes. Single zone valve boxes control the flow of medical gases in a specific area, ensuring efficient management of gas supply for individual rooms or units. In contrast, multiple zone valve boxes are designed to regulate gas distribution across several areas simultaneously, offering greater flexibility and centralized control for hospitals and medical facilities. Both types are essential for optimizing medical gas delivery systems.</p></p>
