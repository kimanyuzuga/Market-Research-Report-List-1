<p><p>The Online Restaurant Reservation System market includes two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises systems are installed locally on a restaurant's hardware, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based systems are hosted remotely, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, as users can access the system from any device with internet connectivity. Both types cater to restaurants seeking efficient reservation management and enhanced customer experience.</p></p>
