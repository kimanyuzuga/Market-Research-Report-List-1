<p><p>The seaplane market comprises three main types: floatplanes, flying boats, and amphibians. Floatplanes are equipped with floats for water landings but require a runway for takeoff. Flying boats have a hull design that allows them to land on water and take off from it without additional gear. Amphibians combine features of both, enabling them to operate on water and land. Each type serves distinct operational needs, catering to specific markets such as tourism, transportation, and emergency services.</p></p>
