<p><p>The camera module adhesives market is categorized into UV curable and non UV curable types. UV curable adhesives cure quickly when exposed to ultraviolet light, offering fast production times and strong bonding for camera modules. These are ideal for high-speed manufacturing environments. Non UV curable adhesives, on the other hand, rely on chemical reactions or heat for curing, providing flexibility in applications where UV exposure is impractical. Both types cater to the demanding requirements of the electronics industry.</p></p>
