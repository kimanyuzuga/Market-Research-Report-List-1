<p><p>Molded glass is a manufacturing process that involves shaping glass by heating it until it becomes pliable, allowing it to be formed into specific designs or shapes using molds. Technological advancements in production techniques are also contributing to market growth, allowing for more intricate designs and reduced production costs. </p><p>Moreover, consumer preferences are shifting toward eco-friendly and reusable materials, further positioning molded glass as a favorable alternative to plastics.
