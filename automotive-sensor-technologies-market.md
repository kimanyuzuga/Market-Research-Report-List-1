<p><p>The automotive sensor technologies market comprises various types that enhance vehicle performance and safety. Rotational motion sensors monitor wheel speed, while chemical and gas sensors detect harmful emissions. Pressure sensors gauge tire and fluid pressures, and angular/linear position sensors track component positioning. Temperature sensors ensure optimal engine operating conditions, whereas mass airflow sensors regulate air intake for efficiency. Accelerometers measure acceleration forces, and image sensors enable advanced driver-assistance systems. Other sensors include ultrasonic and proximity sensors, contributing to overall vehicle intelligence.</p></p>
