<p><p>Polyisobutene (PIB) is categorized into three market types based on molecular weight: low, medium, and high. Low molecular weight PIB is often used in adhesives and sealants due to its flexibility and tackiness. Medium molecular weight PIB finds applications in lubricants and fuel additives, balancing viscosity and stability. High molecular weight PIB is utilized in products like rubber modifiers and coatings, providing enhanced durability and performance. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, contributing to PIB's versatility in various applications.</p></p>
