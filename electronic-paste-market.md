<p><p>The electronic paste market encompasses three main types: conductive paste, resistance paste, and dielectric paste. Conductive paste is primarily used for its excellent electrical conductivity in applications like circuit assembly and electronic components. Resistance paste serves to create resistive elements, critical for precise electrical functions. Dielectric paste, on the other hand, provides electrical insulation, ensuring safe operation of electronic devices. Together, these pastes play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and reliability of electronic products across various industries.</p></p>
