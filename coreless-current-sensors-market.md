<p><p>The Coreless Current Sensors market is primarily divided into two types: Coreless Closed Loop Current Sensors and Coreless Open Loop Current Sensors. Coreless Closed Loop sensors provide high accuracy by using feedback mechanisms to compensate for external influences, making them suitable for precision applications. In contrast, Coreless Open Loop sensors measure current without feedback, offering simpler designs and lower costs, ideal for less demanding applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, industrial automation, and renewable energy.</p></p>
