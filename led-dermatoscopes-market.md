<p><p>The LED dermatoscope market is categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact dermatoscopes. Contact dermatoscopes require direct skin contact to provide enhanced visualization of skin lesions, offering detailed images through the use of various magnifications. In contrast, non-contact dermatoscopes allow for examination without direct skin contact, making them less invasive and more comfortable for patients. Both types leverage advanced LED lighting technology to improve the clarity and accuracy of skin assessments, aiding in the diagnosis of dermatological conditions.</p></p>
