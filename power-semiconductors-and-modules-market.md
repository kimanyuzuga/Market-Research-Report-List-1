<p><p>Power semiconductors and modules are critical components in managing and controlling electrical power in various applications. MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors) are widely used for switching due to their efficiency. Discrete IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors) combine the advantages of bipolar and MOSFET technologies for medium to high power applications. IGBT modules integrate multiple IGBTs and associated components for enhanced performance in industrial electronics. Other market segments include diodes and converters, catering to diverse power management needs across industries.</p></p>
