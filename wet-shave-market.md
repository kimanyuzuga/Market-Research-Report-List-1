Rotary electric shavers feature circular blades that move independently, adapting to facial contours, providing convenience and ease of use. Reciprocating electric shavers utilize oscillating blades for a close shave, often preferred for their efficiency on thicker hair. Each type caters to distinct consumer preferences and grooming needs.</p></p>
