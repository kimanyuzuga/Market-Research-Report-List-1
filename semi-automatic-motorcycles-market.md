<p><p>The semi-automatic motorcycles market comprises primarily two types: Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT) and other transmission types. DCT employs two separate clutches for even gear shifts without manual clutch operation, enhancing performance and ease of use. Other transmission types include traditional automatic and continuously variable transmissions (CVTs), which provide seamless power delivery but may lack the sportier feel of DCT. Each type caters to different rider preferences, focusing on convenience, control, and overall riding experience.</p></p>
