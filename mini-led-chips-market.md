<p><strong>The Mini LED Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip LED</li><li>Vertical Chip LED</li><li>Flip Chip LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mini LED chips market comprises three primary types: Lateral Chip LED, Vertical Chip LED, and Flip Chip LED. Lateral Chip LEDs feature side-emitting designs, suitable for applications requiring thin form factors. Vertical Chip LEDs, characterized by their vertical structure, allow for efficient heat dissipation and improved performance. Flip Chip LEDs utilize a flip structure to connect directly to substrates, enhancing light extraction and thermal management, making them ideal for high-performance lighting solutions. Each type serves unique application needs.</p></p>
