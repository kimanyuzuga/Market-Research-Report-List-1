<p><p>Worm gear reducers are crucial in various mechanical applications, categorized into orthogonal, parallel-shaft, and coaxial types. Orthogonal worm gear reducers feature an input and output shaft that intersect at 90 degrees, ideal for compact designs. Parallel-shaft types maintain aligned shafts, allowing for smoother operation and higher efficiency. Coaxial reducers have shafts aligned along a common axis, minimizing space requirements. Each type offers distinct advantages in torque transmission and space efficiency, suited for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
