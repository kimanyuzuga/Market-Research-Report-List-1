<p><p>The low temperature sensor market comprises various types, including Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) sensors, Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTD), and thermocouples, alongside other sensor types. NTC sensors exhibit decreased resistance as temperature drops, making them suitable for precise measurements. RTDs utilize the change in resistance of metals to gauge temperature changes accurately. Thermocouples generate voltage changes in response to temperature differentials, ideal for high-temperature environments. Other sensor types may include fiber optics and semiconductor-based sensors, each catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
