The market includes two primary types: Passive PTMDs, which operate without external power and rely on gravity and inertia to dissipate energy, and Active PTMDs, which utilize sensors and actuators to adjust their response in real-time for enhanced performance. Both types are crucial in engineering to improve structural stability and occupant comfort in tall buildings and bridges.</p></p>
