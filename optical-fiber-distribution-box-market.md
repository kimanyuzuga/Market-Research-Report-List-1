<p><p>The Optical Fiber Distribution Box market is segmented based on different optical splitters, which distribute signals among multiple fiber connections. The 1:4 optical splitter allows one input to be divided into four outputs, suitable for small networks. The 1:8 optical splitter expands this to eight outputs, ideal for medium-sized setups. The 1:16 optical splitter offers 16 outputs, supporting larger systems, while the 1:32 optical splitter accommodates even broader applications, catering to extensive communication networks. Each type serves specific distribution needs within fiber optic infrastructures.</p></p>
