<p><p>The solder sticks market is categorized primarily into three types: aluminum solder sticks, tin solder sticks, and lead solder sticks. Aluminum solder sticks are used for aluminum alloy joining, offering excellent corrosion resistance. Tin solder sticks are widely utilized in electronics for their good conductivity and low melting points. Lead solder sticks, although less common due to health concerns, provide reliable connections and are favored in specific applications for their superior flow characteristics. Each type caters to different industrial needs and regulatory standards.</p></p>
