<p><p>The ethylene and propylene cracking catalysts market is divided into several types based on their application and composition. CHP (Continuous Hydrothermal Process) catalysts are used for efficient hydrocarbon conversion. CRP (Catalytic Cracking Process) catalysts enhance the cracking of heavier fractions. CIP (Catalytic Intermediate Process) targets specific intermediate compounds for optimized yields. MMC (Molecular Sieve Catalysts) offer high selectivity and activity for targeted reactions. Other catalysts include various proprietary formulations that cater to specific operational needs in cracking processes, enhancing overall efficiency.</p></p>
