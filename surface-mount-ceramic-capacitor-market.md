<p><p>The surface mount ceramic capacitor market is categorized into four types based on capacitance values. Capacitors below 0.1μF are typically used for high-frequency applications and signal filtering. The 0.1μF to 100μF range is popular for general-purpose applications, including decoupling and bypassing in circuit boards. Capacitors in the 100μF to 500μF range are commonly used in power supply filtering and energy storage. Above 500μF capacitors cater to specialized applications requiring high capacitance, such as in power electronic circuits and energy management systems.</p></p>
