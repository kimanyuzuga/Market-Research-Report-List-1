<p><p>The electrically heated glass market primarily consists of laminated glass and hollow glass types. Laminated glass features a polymer interlayer between two glass layers, allowing for effective heating while ensuring safety and structural integrity. Hollow glass, characterized by an air gap between two glass panels, provides enhanced insulation and energy efficiency, making it suitable for larger installations. Both types utilize transparent conductive coatings or embedded heating elements to offer functionalities such as defogging, de-icing, and improved comfort in various applications.</p></p>
