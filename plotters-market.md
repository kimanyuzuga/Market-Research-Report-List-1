<p><p>Plotters are categorized into two main market types: fixed and portable. Fixed plotters are designed for permanent installation in designated areas, often utilized for large-scale printing in industries like architecture and engineering. They offer high precision and larger media handling capacities. In contrast, portable plotters are compact, lightweight, and easily transportable, catering to users who require flexibility. They are ideal for on-site work, such as in construction or design projects, where mobility and convenience are essential.</p></p>
