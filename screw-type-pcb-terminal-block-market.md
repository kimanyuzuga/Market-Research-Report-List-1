<p><p>The Screw-Type PCB Terminal Block market is divided into two main types: Single Layer Wiring and Multilayer Wiring. Single Layer Wiring involves connections on a single circuit board layer, providing simplicity and ease of use for low-density applications. In contrast, Multilayer Wiring supports multiple layers, allowing for more complex designs and higher connection densities. This enables better space utilization and improved electrical performance, making each type suitable for different applications based on the complexity and density of wiring needed.</p></p>
