<p><p>The precast concrete stairs market consists of two primary types: beam-type stairs and slab stairs. Beam-type stairs utilize structural beams to support the stair treads, providing strength and flexibility in design. They are often used in commercial applications due to their durability. Slab stairs, on the other hand, are constructed using solid concrete slabs, offering a seamless and robust appearance. Both types are favored for their quick installation and reduced labor costs, making them popular choices in modern construction.</p></p>
